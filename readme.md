# Gravity

Welcome to Gravity, an innovative app that helps you cultivate gratitude, reflect on your memories, and evaluate your connections. By nurturing these aspects of your life, you can foster a more mindful and fulfilling journey.

## Key Features

- **Gratitude Practice:** Create a daily gratitude journal to recognize the positive aspects of your life. Reflect on what you appreciate and cultivate a sense of well-being.
- **Memory Reflection:** Delve into your past experiences with memory journals and photo albums. Relive special moments and learn from your journey.
- **Connection Evaluation:** Assess your relationships and social interactions. Track your connections to understand their impact on your life.

## How to Use
- **Gratitude Journal:** Start your day by writing down things you're grateful for. You can add text, images, or voice notes.
- **Memory Reflection:** Organize and annotate your memories with text, images, or video. Reflect on these moments for personal growth.
- **Connection Evaluation:** Use the connection tracker to log interactions and relationships. Add notes to track how you feel about these connections.
